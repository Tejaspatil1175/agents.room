# AgentFlow

### Personal AI Helpers for Everyone

**AgentFlow** empowers anyone to create custom AI agents in seconds. No coding, no high monthly fees, and no complex workflows. Just type one sentence and let your personal agent handle the rest.

---

## The Problem
People want personal AI helpers — but every tool is built for companies.
- A **student in shirpur** wants AI to send him cricket scores at 9 PM on WhatsApp.
- A **freelancer** wants research on any topic delivered to his inbox every morning.
- A **job seeker** wants new job listings filtered and sent to Telegram daily.

Until now, no tool existed for this. Platforms like **Zapier, Make, and n8n** require hours of setup, technical knowledge, and cost $50–100/month. They are built for company IT teams, not individuals.

---

##  The Solution: AgentFlow
AgentFlow lets any person type one sentence, create a personal AI agent, set a schedule, and pick their preferred delivery method (**WhatsApp, Email, or Slack**).

- **Zero Coding**: Completely intuitive interface.
- **Zero Complexity**: Set it and forget it.
- **Automated**: Runs every day on your schedule.

---

##  Features
- **One-Sentence Creation**: Define your agent's purpose in natural language.
- **Multi-Channel Delivery**: Receive updates where you already spend your time (WhatsApp / Email / Slack).
- **Scheduled Tasks**: Automate research, writing, finance, health tracking, and more.
- **MERN Stack**: Built with MongoDB, Express.js, React, and Node.js for scalability and performance.

---

##  Vision
A platform where anyone can create a personal AI agent for any task — research, writing, finance, health — set a schedule, connect to their favorite apps, and have it run automatically every day.

---

##  Technical Stack (MERN)
- **Frontend**: React.js
- **Backend**: Node.js & Express.js
- **Database**: MongoDB
- **AI Integration**: Personal Agent Logic
